# Retail-Sales-Performance-Analysis-
This project analysed retail sales and store data to establish key performance indicators (KPIs), identify  profitable trends, and offer actionable business recommendations. 

A data-driven project analyzing retail sales and store data to find key performance insights.

* Total Revenue: ₹103.31M | Profit: ₹26.61M | Margin: 25.77%
* South region leads with 42% of total sales.
* Clothing is most profitable; Furniture needs pricing review.
* January shows highest sales; August the lowest.
* Tools used: Python (Numpy,Pandas,Matplotlib,seaborn, SQL), Power BI, MySQL.
* Includes KPIs, regional & product analysis, and dashboards.
* Goal: Turn raw data into actionable business insights.
* Author: Rohit Singh, 2025.
